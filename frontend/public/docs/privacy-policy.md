# Privacy Policy

**Booking Nest**
Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Booking Nest ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our scheduling and reservation platform at bookingnest. (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Full name and username
- Email address
- Password (stored in hashed form — never in plain text)

### 1.2 Calendar and Reservation Data
When you create a calendar or manage reservations, we collect:
- Calendar name, slug (public URL identifier), and description
- Reservation details submitted by your customers: name, email address, selected date and time, and any optional notes
- Booking history and status updates (confirmed, cancelled, completed)

### 1.4 Usage and Technical Data
We automatically collect:
- IP address and browser type
- Pages visited and features used within the Service
- Device type and operating system
- Session timestamps and error logs

### 1.5 Email Communications
When you interact with our email notifications (booking confirmations, reminders, verification emails), we may track delivery and open status to ensure reliable delivery.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- **Create and manage your account** — authenticate your identity and personalise your experience
- **Operate your booking calendar** — display your public scheduling page and process reservations submitted by your customers
- **Send transactional emails** — booking confirmations, cancellations, reminders, and email address verification
- **Improve the Service** — analyse usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features
- **Communicate with you** — respond to support requests and send service-related announcements
- **Comply with legal obligations** — retain records as required by applicable law

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share data only in the following circumstances:

- **Email service providers** — to send transactional and notification emails on your behalf.
- **Hosting and infrastructure providers** — cloud hosting providers that store and serve the application data under data processing agreements.
- **Legal requirements** — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

We do not share your customers' reservation data with any third party except as required to operate the Service.

---

## 4. Public Calendar Pages

When you create and publish a calendar, your calendar page at `/calendar/{your-slug}` is **publicly accessible**. This page displays:
- Your calendar name and description
- Your available booking slots
- Your username (unless you configure otherwise)

Customers who submit reservations through your public calendar page provide their name, email, and selected time. You are responsible for informing your customers about how their data is used. As the calendar owner, you act as a data controller for your customers' reservation data.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Account and calendar data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- If you delete your account, your personal data and calendars are permanently deleted within 30 days.
- Reservation records may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for backup and legal compliance purposes.
- Payment records are retained as required by financial regulations.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

- Passwords are stored using secure hashing (bcrypt or equivalent)
- Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Database access is restricted to authorised services only
- Email verification is required for all new accounts

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to risk. Please use a strong, unique password and keep your login credentials secure.

Booking Nest uses minimal, functional cookies to:

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
